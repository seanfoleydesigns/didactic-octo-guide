How To Play

Turn all tiles to one color in as few as 7 moves!

The Rules

Click any tile to flip it and its adjacent neighbors. On our 5×5 grid, each position has a different pattern - tiles in the center flip in a plus (+) shape, edge tiles flip in a T shape, and corner tiles flip in an L shape. Your goal is to make all tiles the same color - can you find the 7-move solution?

Tips: Each tile toggles between the current color and black when flipped. Remember that clicking a tile affects multiple tiles at once! Every daily puzzle can be solved in exactly 7 moves - finding that perfect solution is the ultimate challenge!
